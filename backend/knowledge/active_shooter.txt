Active Shooter Procedure (Run – Hide – Fight)

1. Run:
   - If there is a safe escape route, leave your belongings and run.
   - Help others escape if possible, but do not slow yourself down.
   - Keep your hands visible for law enforcement.
2. Hide:
   - If escape is not possible, hide in a room, lock or barricade the door.
   - Turn off lights and silence phones.
   - Stay quiet and out of sight.
3. Fight (last resort):
   - If your life is in immediate danger, attempt to incapacitate the attacker.
   - Use improvised weapons and act with full commitment.
4. When police arrive:
   - Keep your hands visible and empty.
   - Follow their instructions; do not run towards them or shout.
