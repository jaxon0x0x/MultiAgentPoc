Earthquake Procedure

1. During shaking:
   - Indoors: Drop, cover, and hold on under a sturdy table or desk.
   - Stay away from windows, glass, and heavy objects that could fall.
   - Do not use elevators.
2. Outdoors:
   - Move away from buildings, streetlights, and power lines.
   - Drop to the ground and protect your head and neck.
3. In a vehicle:
   - Pull over to a safe location away from bridges, trees, and power lines.
   - Stay inside the vehicle until the shaking stops.
4. After the shaking stops:
   - Check yourself and others for injuries.
   - Evacuate damaged buildings carefully.
   - Be prepared for aftershocks.
   - Avoid using open flames due to possible gas leaks.
