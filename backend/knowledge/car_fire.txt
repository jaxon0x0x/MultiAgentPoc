Car Fire Procedure

1. Pull over safely to the side of the road and stop the vehicle.
2. Turn off the engine and remove the key from the ignition.
3. Evacuate all passengers immediately and move at least 30 meters away from the vehicle.
4. Do not open the hood or trunk; introducing air can make the fire worse.
5. If you have a suitable fire extinguisher and it is safe, use it from a safe distance.
6. Call the fire department and give your exact location.
7. Do not return to the vehicle until authorities declare it safe.
